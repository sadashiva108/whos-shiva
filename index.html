शिव Shiva

Thesis

From remote antiquity, Shiva as adiyogi systematized tantras and yogic practices into a universal system for the purpose of developing and expanding human consciousness to attain the ultimmate goal of moksha. Shiva's system or Shaivism has had an enduring impact on global spirituality as can be found embedded in many of world's religions and spiritual systems. This is evident upon examination of several types of evidence.
